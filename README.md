# Fahamu Kenya

> *Know Who Governs You*

A civic awareness platform that maps Kenya's government structure — every leader, every office, every salary — from the President down to your Village Elder.

## Features

- **Command Tree** — interactive hierarchical explorer of the full government structure across all arms (Executive, Legislature, Judiciary, County, Security, Field Administration, Independent Offices)
- **Salary Insights** — official SRC salary figures for all major positions
- **Government Payroll by Institution** — monthly and annual salary expenditure totals per institution, with per-position breakdown
- **County Explorer** — browse all 47 counties with governor, deputy governor, and senator data
- **MP & MCA Explorer** — all 349 Members of Parliament by county, with clickable detail modals showing salary breakdown, role, and constitutional basis
- **Constitution** — key constitutional references
- **Civic Quiz** — test your knowledge of Kenya's government

## Data Sources

All data is sourced from publicly available Kenyan government sources:

- [parliament.go.ke](https://parliament.go.ke)
- [src.go.ke](https://www.src.go.ke) — Salaries & Remuneration Commission
- [president.go.ke](https://www.president.go.ke)
- [judiciary.go.ke](https://www.judiciary.go.ke)

Salary figures reference **SRC Gazette Notice No. 10346 (9 Aug 2023)** and related notices.
